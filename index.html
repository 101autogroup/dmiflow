FAQ
Common questions, clear answers
How does the AI voice agent actually work?
Our AI listens to each caller, understands their intent using natural language processing, and responds naturally. It can answer FAQs, book appointments, take messages, and route calls—all without human intervention.
Will it sound like a robot?
Not at all. Our AI uses advanced voice synthesis to sound natural and conversational. Most callers won’t realize they’re speaking with an AI.
How long does setup take?
Typically 3–5 business days from kickoff to go-live. We handle the AI build, you just provide info about your business and review the final product.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
Absolutely. We set up call forwarding from your existing number so nothing changes for your customers. No new numbers needed.
What if a caller needs a real person?
The AI can detect when a caller needs human assistance and will transfer the call to your team, take a message, or flag it for urgent follow-up—your choice.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month. Plus we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee so you can try it risk-free.
What integrations do you support?
We integrate with popular CRMs, calendar systems, and business tools. During onboarding, we’ll identify the best integrations for your workflow.
